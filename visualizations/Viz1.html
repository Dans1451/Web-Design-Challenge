Latitude Vs Max Temperature

Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temperature
Visualizations

This plot represents the relationship between a city's Latitude and its maximum temperature recorded on the sample day. The relationship shows that closer a city is to Latitude 0 (The Equator), the higher likely hood of a hotter day. There is a noticable trend for a sharper drop off in temperatures for coties in the northern hemisphere though.
